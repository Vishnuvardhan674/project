upma

About vadaa


Upma is a delicious South Indian recipe and is a perfect breakfast option for many. It is an easy-to-make dish that can be prepared in under 20 minutes. There are several varieties of upma that you can prepare, however, it is one of the simplest ones. These days, you can easily find ready-to-eat upma, but the packaged ones are full of preservatives. Prepare a healthy and fresh meal for yourself by trying out this recipe.


Ingredirents


  1. 1 cup semolina
  2. salt as required
  3. 1 onion
  4. 2 tablespoon ghee
  5. 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  6. 10 curry leaves
  7. 1 green chilli
  8. 1 1/4 cup boiling wate

Preparetation


Step 1 Roast the sooji To prepare this delicious dish, peel the onion and potato, and finely chop them in separate bowls. Next, put a pan over low flame and dry roast the semolina in it. Once done, transfer to a bowl and keep aside until required.


Step 2 Next, put the boiled kernels in a grinder and grind them. Now, take a bowl and add grinded corn kernels, ginger paste, chopped green chillies, cilantro, black pepper and salt. Mix them well.


Step 2 Prepare the tempering Then, put the same pan over medium flame and melt ghee in it. Once melted, add mustard seeds in it along with curry leaves and green chilli. Let them temper for a few seconds and then add chopped onion in it. Saute for a minute and then add chopped potato. Add salt as per taste. Cover the pan and let the ingredients cook for a minute


Step 3 Boil the water Meanwhile, boil water in a saucepan over medium flame. You can also use an electric kettle to boil the water till the potatoes are being cooked.


Step 4 Cook and serve Add the roasted semolina to the veggies and mix well. Now quickly add the boiled water to the sooji and mix well. Cover the pan with its lid and let the upma cook for a minute or two. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot.
