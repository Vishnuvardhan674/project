vadaa

About vadaa


Famous for its variety of spices used in different foods, all the recipes of southern cuisine are supremely delicious. Of all the dishes, dosa is something that we all love to have. Over the years several variations of dosa have come, however, if you are someone who enjoys authentic flavours then you should definitely try this easy recipe!


Ingredirents


  1. 3 tablespoon cilantro
  2. 8 slices bread slices
  3. 4 cup American corn kernels
  4. 4 teaspoon ginger paste
  5. salt as required
  6. 3 green chilli
  7. 3 green chilli
  8. water as required

Preparetation


Step 1 To prepare this dish, wash the corn kernels under running water and put them in a pressure cooker. Pour water into the cooker and boil the corn kernels until tender. On the other hand, finely chop cilantro and green chillies and keep them aside. Also, mash the bread slices.


Step 2 Next, put the boiled kernels in a grinder and grind them. Now, take a bowl and add grinded corn kernels, ginger paste, chopped green chillies, cilantro, black pepper and salt. Mix them well.


Step 3 Now, add the mashed bread into the bowl and mix them. Take small portions of the mixture and shape them into a vada


Step 4 Next, place a frying pan over medium flame and pour oil in it. Once the oil is heated, deep fry the vadas until golden brown in colour from both the sides. Serve!
